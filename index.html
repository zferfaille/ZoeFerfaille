Second year placement - Research into eating behavior

Internship Research FoodScience Immersion Psycho Python RStudio

I was in charge of a research project on eating behavior. It was a call for expressions of interest from the Lille metropolitan area, in collaboration with JUNIA and Anthropo-Lab. JUNIA develops expertise and services around sensory analysis, while Anthropo-Lab is an experimental anthropology laboratory that aims to understand and develop human behavior. My role was to identify physiological markers that reflect appreciation and emotions during an act of consumption. My tasks were to carry out a literature review, set up an experimental protocol in an immersive room, analyze the results and present them.
